Bank robbery

Petty theft, theft and drug dealing alone won't get you far in Los Santos. To get rich, Michael, Franklin and Trevor will work together on robberies - large missions with multiple parts. The success of such assignments lies in careful preparation, selection of participants and impeccable execution. The main thing in GTA V is money. And although the game has enough ways to get and spend cash, you can only make a big fortune on robberies.

David «Dave» Norton

is an agent of the Federal Reserve Bank and a good friend of Michael. He got Michael into a witness protection program and writes letters to Trevor disguised as Brad. Voiced by Julian Gamble

Lester Crest

is an old friend of Michael and a mastermind while planning robberies with Michael, Franklin and Trevor. He also issues assassination missions to Franklin.

Tracey De Santa

Daughter of Michael and Amanda and sister of Jimmy De Santa. She has a very difficult relationship with her parents, especially her mother, who does not like Tracy's boyfriend, calling him a vagabond. Her relationship with her father is also far from perfect.

Franklin Clinton

a young African American looking to pursue a successful career in the Los Santos criminal underworld. He works as a prop for the owner of a luxury car dealership, but his ambition makes him leave the business. Franklin soon meets Michael, a once successful bank robber, and they develop a very close relationship. Together with Trevor, they commit a series of robberies, soon becoming rich.

Michael De Santa

Is a retired bank robber who, after a deal with FIB, moved with his family to Los Santos, where they live in a nice mansion. Unfortunately, his wife, Amanda, mercilessly spends the money that Michael has stolen over the years. This, as well as difficult relationships with family members and huge debts force him to return to criminal life once again, this time, relying on his old partner Trevor Phillips, as well as new acquaintance Franklin Clinton.

Trevor Philips

Is a mentally unstable redneck and former bank robber along with his then partner Michael Townley. A former military pilot, Trevor moved to San Andreas, where he founded Trevor Philips Enterprises, which mainly deals with weapons and drugs.

GTA V

Grand Theft Auto V is an action-adventure game played from either a third-person or first-person perspective. Players complete missions—linear scenarios with set objectives—to progress through the story. Outside of the missions, players may freely roam the open world.
